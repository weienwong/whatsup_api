About WhatsUp


WhatsUp is a university-based event discovery platform that allows everyone to find out about events happening / hosted at the university!

This web app / platform is aiming to become something like the Reddit or HackerNews for events.

Users can look for events that are of a Event Type, so that they can find events they would want to attend that fits their interest. They can also filter events based on the timeframe of their choosing.

This app is constantly growing, and new features are constantly being added to enhance the usability and power of this platform.

If you have any events you would like to posted on this platform, or have any questions, comments, and/or concerns, please email me at: whatsup@gmail.com
