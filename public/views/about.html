About WhatsUp


WhatsUp is a university-based event discovery platform that allows everyone to find out about events happening / hosted at the university!

This web app / platform is aiming to become something like the Reddit or HackerNews for events.

This app is constantly growing, and new features are constantly being added to enhance the usability and power of this platform.

About Me

What I think is awesome, is the ability to use technology to help others. I run a side project, refurbishing computers and donating them to low-income families in the Kitchener-Waterloo Region as well as inner-city neighborhoods in Toronto. Over the past 5 years, we have refurbished and donated over 70 computers.
